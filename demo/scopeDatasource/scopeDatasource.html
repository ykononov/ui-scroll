Datasource on scope (not as service)

As it follows from documentation the directive will first look for a property with the given name (datasource) on its $scope. 
<li ui-scroll="item in datasource">{{item}}</li>
So you need to declare datasource object on scope of your controller and define method get on it. 
$scope.datasource = {}
